My day to day life looks a little like this

Making Art
Drawign and painting have been some of my go to's when it comes to havign a quiet day to relax and reflect. With a preference of sketching realistic animals, I find myself taking pictures of the occasional squirrel, rabbit, or even deer that I may come across, just to recreate it on paper.

Playing the Bass
I have always had an interest in music and been inspired by the skills shown in playing such instruments. This has led me to trying all kinds of instruments, and for now I have settled with the Bass. Currently, I am self-taught on what I know so far, finding my own resources where I can.

Enjoying Videogames
Enjoyment of the story-telling, game mechanics, or something as simple as the soundtrack of a videogame is something that comes easily to me. With a broad taste in videogames, from action packed games in the souls-like genre, to more relaxing and satisfying ones in the rpg genre, there is never a lack of games for me to try out.
